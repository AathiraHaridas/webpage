UI Design Project
01
OBJECTIVE
The objective is to create an online furniture shopping app, that helps users shop conveniently anywhere. Users will be provided with the advantage of shopping online without entering a physical shop. All products will be given different colours and relevant information, to ensure transparency between seller and buyer.
02
05
USER STORIES & FLOW DIAGRAM
The app can be used at home, when decorating a room or at a shop looking for a similar design or different colour or a cheaper price.
WHEN
The app will have database of different furnitures and their available colours. It will also provide the high rated products, latest collections and design ideas for each room.

WHAT
For users who do not have the time to go to a physical shop and cannot go to a shop, because of the an extraordinary situation, like the Coronavirus pandemic. This also applies to people looking for a variety and are moving into a new home or redecorating.

WHO
BRANDING & LOGO
Since this an online shopping app, the logo will represent an evolved version of box or package, and finally to a chair.
The chair implies a furniture store.  The chosen name “dekor”  simply means decor in German.

The logo and the name will always be placed in a white space.

The text can be placed to the right or below the logo.
