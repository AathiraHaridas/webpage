01
Solution
An app that offers simple methods to meditation and visualization. To attain this goal, features like - a growth process is provided, stories to tackle certain day to day problems, a visionboard to inspire and aspire where a person wants to be and wants in the future, meditation that guides to peace and finally, sleep to rectify insomnia and depression.
Problem
There are many videos and apps that guide you to meditation, but there aren’t apps that focus on visualization techniques. Visualization is the key for finding peace and showering positivity. This in turn allows anyone to stay healthy and happy.
“Each one of us has the potential of being given a gift by the Divine, which results in the body and soul becoming unified… a great many people will experience the blessings of the Divine…Through it the human being will first be made healthy, and then peace of mind and joy in life will be increased.
- Dr. Mikao Usui
02
WHEN
It can be used at any time. It can be at work during a lunch time, or may be at home in the morning or evening. It will depend upon the user and his/her situation.
WHAT
The app will have a set of videos and audios. Users can choose to  watch animated or feel good videos, or listen to soundscapes or guided meditations and visualizations. It will also include gratitude and affirmation audios and stories to guide the user to a peaceful state.  
WHO
People who are seeking to find peace, or take a break from work & stress, or anyone wants to lead a mindful life. There are no age restrictions, it applies to anyone who wants to meditate or learn meditation and visualization.

09
Final Screens
The Daydream sign in and sign up is kept simple and easy to use. The idea is to wait for what’s coming next.
The onboarding screens are set to be colorful, to bring in excitement and joy. The first onboarding screen guides us to a simple breath in and breath out. The colours of the sun is used to bring in warmth and happiness in the first screen. The second and the third screen gives a slight glimpse into how visualization looks like. The fourth screen gives a guidance on how to attain your goals and dreams.
Five screens are used for browsing : -

Growth -  a mindful routine for each day. It presents 3 activities, namely meditation, visualization and story. It is a slow process of helping user lead a mindful life,
Stories - guidance and stories regarding well being. It also contains certain stories related to gratitude & affirmations. All of them will be presented as videos to help the user visualise better.
Visionboard - a digital scrapbook for the goals you want to attain. User can create this visonboard and daily view it for manifestation of their goals.
Meditation - guidance and soundscapes for peace and harmony. These are audio meditations to help the user achieve a serene state.
Sleep - guidance, stories and soundscapes for a sound sleep. There are both audio and video guidance to help user to sleep peacefully.
When clicked on Desert waves on the Sleep screen, the soundscape - with night time sounds in a desert can be heard.
The Visionboard allows you to create your digital scrapbook. When clicked on “Add your Visionboard”, the user  can type the title of the of the Visionboard and then go to the “select a layout” screen.  After choosing a layout, user goes to the “create your visionboard” screen. Here the user can add pictures or text, add colors, resize the image/text/layout and even add an animation. Once “Done” is clicked, it will be saved on the visionboard screen.
